# EduLove - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

EduLove ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name, email address, date of birth
- University and course information
- Profile information (bio, interests, photos)
- Communication data (messages, reports)

### Automatic Information
- Device information (IP address, browser type)
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

### Student Verification Data
- Student ID documents (encrypted, securely stored)
- University email verification records

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- Account creation and authentication
- Matching and profile discovery
- Communication between users
- Safety and security purposes
- Moderation and abuse prevention
- Service improvement
- Legal compliance

## 4. Student Verification

- Student ID documents are stored securely and encrypted
- Only authorized staff can access verification documents
- Documents are retained for 90 days after verification approval
- After 90 days, documents are securely deleted

## 5. Data Sharing

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your personal data
- Share data with third parties for marketing
- Share data without your consent (except as legally required)

**We MAY share data:**
- With service providers (hosting, analytics)
- For legal compliance and safety
- With law enforcement (if legally required)

## 6. Data Security

We implement:
- HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
- Password hashing with bcrypt
- Secure database encryption
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

## 7. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request data deletion (right to be forgotten)
- Port your data (data portability)
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@edulove.com

## 8. Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days
- **Messages:** Retained for 180 days, then archived
- **Verification documents:** Deleted after 90 days of approval

## 9. Cookies

We use cookies to:
- Maintain login sessions
- Remember preferences
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## 10. Third-Party Services

Our service may include:
- Cloudinary (image storage)
- MongoDB Atlas (database)
- Vercel/Netlify (hosting)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 11. Children's Privacy

EduLove is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of data from users under 18, we will delete it immediately.

## 12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of EduLove constitutes acceptance of updates.
